Welcome to Weather Visualizations


On this Website you will find a variety of plots comparing a random sample of cities' latitude to various other weather conditions. The underlying data used to perform this analysis was obtained from the OpenWeatherMap API. Information from over over 500 cities was pulled and cleansed. After assembling the dataset, we used Pandas & Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather conditions vs. latitude.


Factors considered in this analysis include: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. This site provides the source data as a downloadble CSV, various visualizations created as part of the analysis along with explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
